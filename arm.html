Multifunctional Robotic Arm

A versatile robotic arm designed for multiple applications including pick-and-place, drawing, and object manipulation.

This project showcases a custom-built multifunctional robotic arm capable of performing a variety of tasks. The arm was designed and fabricated using 3D printed components and off-the-shelf servo motors, demonstrating the integration of mechanical design, electronics, and programming.

The robotic arm features multiple degrees of freedom and can be controlled both manually and through pre-programmed sequences, making it suitable for educational demonstrations and practical applications.
