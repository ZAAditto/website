Remote-Controlled Soccer Robot

In Bangladesh, there's a well-known competition where robots compete in a soccer match, trying to score goals against each other. I developed a robot specifically for this contest, and its unique feature is it’s hand-crafted controller. The joystick's circuit is entirely handmade. A Bluetooth module is used to control the soccer bot. To ensure robustness against opponents, I first designed the robot in CAD software (Autodesk Fusion 360). Following these designs the robot is constructed. It is powered by four high-torque motors.
